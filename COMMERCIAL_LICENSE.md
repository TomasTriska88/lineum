# Commercial License & Exceptions

Lineum Core is publicly governed by the AGPLv3 license to ensure scientific reproducibility and open-source transparency. 

However, we offer **Commercial Licenses and Exceptions** for enterprise entities, research labs, or private startups wishing to integrate the Lineum Core physics engine into a proprietary, closed-source product or internal SaaS infrastructure without the strict "copyleft" and source-sharing requirements of the AGPLv3.

### How to Apply
To discuss a commercial exception, please send us an email outlining:
1. **Your Use-Case:** A brief description of how Lineum Core will be utilized.
2. **Architecture:** Whether this is a SaaS deployment, an embedded simulation (e.g., Unity/Unreal), or an internal B2B tool.
3. **Closed-Source Requirements:** Any specific source-code carve-outs or proprietary integrations you need to protect.

### What We Provide
Upon agreement, we typically deliver:
- A formal legal waiver exempting your organization from the AGPLv3 distribution clauses.
- Targeted integration support and early access to upcoming features.
