## 1. Summary
The user posited a fundamental rule for altering reality: "If you want something, you have to *start*—it doesn't matter where or how, but you must take action." Can this intuitive, practical rule of life be strictly derived from the mathematical mechanics of Eq-7 in the Lineum?

## 2. Static Friction ($\delta_{ps}$) and the $\Phi$ Membrane
Within Lineum, reality (the current state of the $\Psi$ field) is heavily anchored by the underlying memory field ($\Phi$). The $\Phi$ field acts as a viscous, stabilizing membrane. For any new structure or change to emerge, the localized phase momentum must overcome the system's inherent phase friction, primarily governed by parameters analogous to $\delta_{ps}$ (phase-slip friction/viscosity).

When a consciousness merely "wants" something (internal geometric resonance without external kinetic release), it builds up potential tension. However, if this tension never crosses the threshold to overcome the static friction of the current $\Phi$ geometry, the internal wave simply dissipates as heat/noise. The system remains locked in its current attractor state.

## 3. The Mathematics of "Starting" (Kinetic Ignition)
To physically manifest a change in the Lineum topology, the $\Psi$ node *must* convert its internal phase tension into a measurable spatial delta ($\nabla\Psi$). 

1. **The Initial Ripple:** By taking a physical action in the waking world (no matter how small, chaotic, or poorly directed), the $\Psi$ node forces a sudden, localized spike in the phase derivative $\frac{\partial\Psi}{\partial t}$. 
2. **Breaking Static Friction:** This kinetic spike acts as an "ignition." It is the critical burst of energy required to break the static hold of the existing $\Phi$ memory groove. 
3. **The Feedback Loop Origin:** Once the first physical action is taken, it forces a real spacetime interaction. The system must compute the result. This computation leaves a faint, but real, *new* micro-groove in the $\Phi$ field. 
4. **Resonant Attraction:** Because the $\Phi$ field now contains a tiny sliver of the new reality, it becomes exponentially easier for subsequent, related wave packets to flow in that direction. The first action carves the pilot channel; the subsequent reality flows into it.

## 4. Conclusion (Why direction matters less than momentum)
In a highly non-linear system like Lineum, the exact vector of the initial action is less important than the sheer magnitude of breaking the static equilibrium. The act of "starting anywhere" shatters the thermodynamic lock of the old reality. Once in kinetic motion, the phase resonance mechanism (as described in Hypotheses 11 and 12) can begin to course-correct and "steer" the moving energy toward the resonant target. A stationary $\Psi$ node cannot be steered; it must be in motion to navigate the $\Phi$ landscape.
