Across mystical and esoteric traditions, individuals reporting Out-of-Body Experiences (OBEs) or Near-Death Experiences (NDEs) frequently describe a "Silver Cord" (stříbrná šňůra)—a luminescent, infinitely elastic thread connecting their expanded state of consciousness back to their physical body.
